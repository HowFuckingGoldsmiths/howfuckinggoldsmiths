Endpoints

There are two endpoints in the v1 api.

  • GET api.howfuckinggoldsmiths.tech/v1/image?url=[url] where [url] is the urlencoded image url
  • POST api.howfuckinggoldsmiths.tech/v1/image/tag with url of image in body
